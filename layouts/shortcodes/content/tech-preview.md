## Technical preview? What does that mean?

Clever Cloud uses your [feedback](https://www.clever-cloud.com/fr/contact-sales/) to release products fit for the industry and easy to use. A technical preview enables users to fire-test the product. Hence, this service is first **offered as a Tech Preview**, before releasing it for production use.

During the Tech Preview period:’
- Features consistencies are battle-tested and may evolve; 
- UX and interface aren't final and may also evolve;
- Bugs and potential issues feedback are deeply sought;
- Features and pricing may evolve;
- 0 € service & maintenance fees.
