We updated Redis™ to [release 8.2.3](https://github.com/redis/redis/releases/tag/8.2.3). It brings bug fixes and security patch for [CVE-2025-62507](https://nvd.nist.gov/vuln/detail/CVE-2025-62507). Redis™ 8.2.3 is available for new add-ons. Those already deployed can upgrade through migration.
