Our [Matomo](https://matomo.org/) add-on has been updated to version `5.9.0` which is now used by default. You can enable "Enforce compliance" to automatically [apply CNIL-compliant](https://matomo.org/faq/how-to/how-do-i-configure-matomo-without-tracking-consent-for-french-visitors-cnil-exemption/) settings to your sites, without manual configuration. A dedicated page lets you create, edit and organise all your segments in one place, accessible from the Visitors menu and segment selector. The updated calendar adds new date presets for frequently used reporting periods.

This release also brings security enhancements such as multi-session sign-out, the ability to export a dashboard to scheduled reports, and various bug fixes across segment handling, visitor mapping and database optimization. No major database upgrade is required.

You can deploy this release from our [Console](https://console.clever-cloud.com) or [Clever Tools](/doc/cli/). Existing customers' add-ons are already up-to-date.
